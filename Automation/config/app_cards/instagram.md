# Instagram App Guide (com.instagram.android)

## UI Layout
- **Navigation Bar:** Bottom of screen. Contains Home, Search, Create (+), Reels, Profile.
- **Create Button:** usually the Plus (+) icon in the center or bottom bar.
- **Next/Share Buttons:** Almost always in the **top-right** corner or a large **blue button** at the bottom.

## Workflow: Create Reel
To upload a new reel, follow this exact sequence:
1. Tap the **Create (+)** button on the bottom bar.
2. Select **"REEL"** from the bottom slider (swipe right if needed).
3. Tap the **Gallery thumbnail** (bottom-left square) to open phone media.
4. Tap the **First Item** (top-left in grid) to select the latest video.
5. Tap **"Next"** (top-right).
6. Tap **"Next"** again to skip the editing screen.
7. **Caption Screen:**
   - Tap the field that says "Write a caption..."
   - **Action:** Type the caption you generated based on the user's goal.
   - **CRITICAL FINAL STEP:** Look for the blue **"Share"** button at the bottom-right or the **"Share"** text at the top-right. If a popup asks "Share to Facebook?", tap "Share to Instagram only".

## Troubleshooting
- If you don't see "Next", look for a blue arrow icon (->).
- If the keyboard blocks the "Share" button, tap the "Back" system button once to hide the keyboard.
